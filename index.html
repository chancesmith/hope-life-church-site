Welcome

Hope Life Church is a warm, close-knit congregation in Jackson, Tennessee. We are committed to knowing God, caring for one another, and sharing the hope of Jesus Christ with our community.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or looking for a church home, we would love to have you join us.

What We Believe

We are a Bible-believing, Christ-centered church. Our faith is rooted in the historic Christian faith as summarized below.

  • The Bible — We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God and our final guide for faith and life.
  • God — We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus Christ — We believe in the full deity and full humanity of Jesus Christ, his atoning death, and his bodily resurrection.
  • Salvation — We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.
  • The Church — We believe the local church is called to worship, disciple, and serve — reflecting Christ's love to the world.
  • Eternity — We believe in the resurrection of the dead and in eternal life for all who trust in Christ.
