Welcome to Hope Life Church

Hope Life Church is a warm, welcoming community of believers committed to growing in faith and serving our Jackson neighbors. Whether you're visiting for the first time or you've been here for years, we'd love to see you in person!

Our Mission: To love God wholeheartedly, love one another deeply, and share Christ's hope with our community.

Our Vision: To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Jackson—a place where everyone feels valued, supported, and connected to Jesus Christ.

With approximately 50 active members, we pride ourselves on knowing and caring for one another. You'll find us genuinely interested in your life and your story.

Our Services

Join us for worship! We offer a mix of traditional and contemporary worship in a relaxed, welcoming environment.

Sunday Worship Service

Time: 10:00 AM – 11:15 AM

Location: Main sanctuary

What to Expect: Worship, prayer, Scripture teaching, and community. Coffee and fellowship time follows the service.

Wednesday Evening Prayer & Study

Time: 6:30 PM – 7:45 PM

Location: Fellowship hall

What to Expect: Group prayer, Bible study, and time to deepen relationships with others in the church.

Sunday School

Time: 9:00 AM (before main service)

Location: Various classrooms

What to Expect: Classes for all ages—kids, teens, young adults, and adults. Find the right fit for your age group!

First time visiting? No need to dress up, bring anything, or know what to expect—just come as you are. We're here to welcome you!

Getting Here

Parking: Free, ample parking available in the front and side lots. Accessible parking is near the main entrance.

Accessibility: Our facility is wheelchair accessible. Accessible restrooms are available.

Children: Nursery care is available during Sunday worship service for children ages 0–3.
